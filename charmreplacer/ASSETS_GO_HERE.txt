Place your custom assets in this folder:

  Skin.png            ← Your custom phone texture
  Skin_Emission.png   ← Emission map (optional)
  hatbundle           ← Your custom charm mesh bundle
  Charms/
    PlayerName.hatbundle  ← Multiplayer charm bundles (one per player)
  Skins/
    PlayerName.png        ← Multiplayer skins (one per player)
    PlayerName_emission.png ← Emission variant (optional)
