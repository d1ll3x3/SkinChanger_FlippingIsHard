How do I find my Steam name?
  1. Open Steam and click your name (top-right) → View my profile.
  2. The big name at the top is your display name.
  3. Type it exactly as it appears, including caps and symbols — it must match the name other players see in-game.

Still stuck? Open steamid.xyz — it shows your name and IDs in one place.

How do I find my Steam ID?
  1. Open your Steam profile in a browser.
  2. Copy the page URL and paste it in the box above, then press Detect (works for both /id/yourname and /profiles/7656119… URLs).
  3. If your URL looks like /profiles/7656119…, the long number is your Steam ID.

Can't get it? Open steamid.xyz, copy the SteamID64 (17 digits), and paste it in the Steam ID box.
